About Me

Hello! I'm Shaheer Masood. I solve problems by building things that make sense.

I'm a data analyst who's equally comfortable writing SQL queries and sketching wireframes. My approach is pretty simple: understand what people actually need, then build something clean and functional that delivers it.

I got into analytics because I liked the puzzle of turning messy data into clear answers. I stayed because I realized the best solutions aren't just about finding insights—they're about building systems that make those insights accessible and useful. Whether that's automating a painful manual process, designing a dashboard someone will actually use, or creating a planning tool that saves hours every week, I care about impact over complexity.

Before data, I ran a small clothing brand. I handled everything—design, manufacturing, marketing, the works. It taught me how to move fast, think creatively under constraints, and that good design isn't decoration, it's clarity. Those lessons still show up in how I approach analytics work. I work in Python, SQL, and whatever tool fits the problem. Based in Chicago, studied at UIC, and always learning something new
