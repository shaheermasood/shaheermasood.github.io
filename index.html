(About Me)

I'm a data analyst who's equally comfortable writing SQL queries and sketching wireframes. My approach is simple: understand what people actually need, then build something clean and functional that delivers it.

Whether that's automating a painful manual process, designing a dashboard someone will actually use, or creating a planning tool that saves hours every week. I care about impact over complexity.

Before data, I ran a small clothing brand. It taught me how to move fast, think creatively under constraints, and that good design isn't decoration it's clarity and user accessibility.

Systems & Automation

Product Thinking
